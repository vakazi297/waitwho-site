PimEyes — Opt-Out Guide

Want your face removed from PimEyes results? Here's how to submit an opt-out request and what to expect.

PimEyes provides a published opt-out/removal process for eligible requests. Follow their current official steps carefully.

Official Opt-Out Link

Open the PimEyes Opt-Out Request Form →

What To Do First

  1. Use the Official PimEyes Opt-Out Page — Start from PimEyes' own website so you reach the latest request form.
  2. Complete Verification — They may require identity or ownership verification before processing removal requests.
  3. Submit Clear Information — Provide requested details accurately and include any relevant result links.
  4. Allow Processing Time — Some requests are reviewed manually and may take time.
  5. Recheck Later — After approval, results may take time to refresh.

Privacy Tip

Use a dedicated email for removal requests when possible to keep personal accounts separate.

Stay Ahead

Even after one removal, new images can surface later. Premium members get recurring rescans and alerts for newly detected matches.
