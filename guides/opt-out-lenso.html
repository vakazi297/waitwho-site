Lenso.ai — Opt-Out Guide

Want your image removed from Lenso.ai results? Here's how to use their opt-out process and protect future visibility.

Lenso.ai may provide removal, privacy, or data-request options through its official website or help center. Always use their latest published process.

Official Opt-Out Link

Open the Lenso.ai Opt-Out Page →

What To Do First

  1. Start on the Official Lenso.ai Site — Use their help, privacy, or support pages to find the current request form.
  2. Gather Evidence — Save screenshots and copy result links before submitting a request.
  3. Complete Verification — They may ask you to confirm identity or ownership of the image.
  4. Submit Accurate Details — Include requested URLs and any supporting information clearly.
  5. Check Back Later — Approved removals may take time to update in search results.

Privacy Tip

Use separate usernames and profile photos across platforms to reduce easy identity linking.

Stay Ahead

One removal doesn't stop future indexing. Premium members get recurring rescans and alerts for newly detected matches.
