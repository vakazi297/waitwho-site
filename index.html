FREQUENTLY ASKED

Answers before you ask.

The twelve most common questions about how WaitWHO works, what it doesn't do, and what happens to your photo.

What is WaitWHO?

WaitWHO is a face-search app. Upload a photo and we search publicly available images across the web for visually similar matches, so you can review what may be publicly visible.

Is WaitWHO available on iPhone?

Yes — WaitWHO is currently iPhone-only. Android support is planned for Summer 2026.

What kind of websites are searched?

Results may include publicly available pages such as social profiles, articles, image platforms, forums, archived pages, and other indexed public sources.

Is WaitWHO searching private accounts?

No. WaitWHO only searches publicly available sources. Private accounts, locked content, and anything behind a login are not searched.

What happens to my photo after the scan?

Your uploaded image is used to run the search and isn't kept long-term. Once the scan completes, the image is purged from our systems. We never sell photos, add them to a public index, or use them to train models.

Does WaitWHO remove content for me?

No. WaitWHO is a search and awareness tool — it doesn't file takedowns or remove content on your behalf. The in-app guides walk you through the options available directly from the hosting platform.

Why should I try multiple photos?

Different photos can produce different results. Lighting, age, hairstyle, angle, glasses, and how much of the face is visible all affect similarity matching.

How do I find old photos of myself online?

Try both recent and older photos in WaitWHO and compare the results. Older photos can surface archived pages that newer ones miss, and vice versa.

Why did my results change later?

The web changes constantly. New pages get indexed, old pages disappear, and the public sources we search update over time — so the same photo can return different matches on different days.

What does Premium include?

Premium plans may include recurring rescans, alerts when new possible matches are detected, additional included searches, and expanded monitoring tools.

Will I get alerts if something new appears?

Premium users may receive notifications when new possible matches are detected during scheduled rescans.

What if I find a photo I don't want online?

WaitWHO includes guides covering common removal requests, reporting steps, and opt-out forms for major search indexes and face-search services.
